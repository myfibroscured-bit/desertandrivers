You don't have to manage it forever

You've been told to just manage it.
I'm here to tell you — there's another way.

For ten years, fibromyalgia ran my life. At my worst I was housebound, taking 60+ tablets a day, unable to cut a piece of cake without my hands giving out. Doctors told me there was no cure — only management. I refused to believe that was the final word.

Today I'm medication-free, symptom-free, and living a life that exceeds anything I imagined back then — as a digital nomad, doing work I love. Through My Fibro Coach, I share the exact holistic roadmap that got me here.
