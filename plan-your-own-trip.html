Custom & Bespoke

Your route is built, not sold.

Desert & River journeys aren’t fixed packages. We start with your available time (1–30 days), your interests, and your comfort level — then design a route that respects conditions, permissions, and the realities of the land.

How it works
1) Choose places
Browse the “Places to Visit” list and note what calls you.
2) Book a call
We’ll discuss pacing, season, and practicalities.
3) We design the route
Logistics, camps, food, vehicles, and permits are planned carefully.
4) Travel with presence
The land sets the pace. We adapt to conditions with experience and care.
A note on pricing

Approximate pricing is around €300 per day (varies with group size and logistics). This isn’t a luxury markup — it’s a filter that protects quality, ethics, and integrity.
