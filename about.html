About Us

Quiet confidence. Real relationship with land and people.

Journeys Beyond the Ordinary

Desert & River exists for travellers who want depth — not a checklist. Our work is deliberately selective and relationship-based, designed around reverence for land and history, protection of fragile environments, and long-term trust with Bedouin communities.

We operate with permits, compliance, insurance, and duty of care in place — and we plan with abundant preparation: vehicles, fuel, food, water, and contingency are always part of responsible desert work.

A note on pace

Silence, heat, bumpy travel, and simplicity are part of the reality. We don’t romanticise it — we respect it. The land, conditions, and guide wisdom determine decisions.

What our work protects

Fragile environments

We actively limit access, keep groups small, and organise clean-up trips when needed.

Bedouin relationships

We travel by permission, not entitlement — and we give back materially and respectfully.

Integrity of the experience

No daily departures, no spectacle. Presence and listening come first.

Safety and duty of care

Permits, planning, and abundant preparation are non-negotiable.
