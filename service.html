Plan Your Own Trip

Custom and bespoke — from 1 day to 30 days.

This isn’t a package page.

Every Desert & River journey is built around the guest, season, conditions, and permissions on the ground. We use a small number of “starting points” (1 day, 6 days, 30 days) only to help you imagine scale — not to lock you into a fixed itinerary.

The land determines the day. We move with restraint, and we plan responsibly: vehicles, fuel, food, water, and contingency are abundant by design. Capacity is limited (around 30 journeys per year) to protect relationships and places.

A note on price

Around €300 per day is a filter and a form of protection — not a luxury markup. Lowering price would reduce care, preparation, and ethical practice.

1) Choose your timeframe

From a single day to a month. We’ll advise what’s realistic for your style and season.

2) Choose your places

Use our “Places to Visit” list to explore options. We’ll recommend what fits together well.

3) Decide on pace

Fewer stops, more time. We optimise for depth, not quantity.

4) We design the route

We build a responsible itinerary around permissions, conditions, and guest readiness.

Quick guidance

If you want the best experience, arrive willing to slow down. These journeys suit guests who value:

  • Presence over stimulation
  • Listening over “highlights”
  • Care over convenience
  • Permission over entitlement

If you need speed, control, or constant activity, we may not be the right fit — and that’s okay.
